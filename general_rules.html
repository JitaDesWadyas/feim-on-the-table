Armor & Damage Reduction

  • Armor doesn’t make you harder to hit; it reduces physical damage (DR).
  • Attack targets a body part; only the armor on that part applies.
  • Total DR (sum) is used for generic hits without location.
  • Magic damage usually ignores DR.
