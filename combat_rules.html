Combat Rules

Round Structure

  • Each round represents exactly 3 seconds of in‑world time.
  • Every character gains 3 seconds of TIME to spend on actions.
  • Actions cost TIME and usually Stamina or Mana.
  • After you perform one action, control passes immediately to the combatant with the highest TIME remaining. Repeat until all participants have 0 s left.

Defense Is Active

No passive AC. To avoid or mitigate damage you must spend TIME and Stamina/Mana on a defensive skill (Block, Parry, Dodge, etc.). Failing to act means you take the hit.

Initiative Tie‑Break

  1. Compare DEX + action TIME (lower total acts first).
  2. If still tied, roll 1d6.
