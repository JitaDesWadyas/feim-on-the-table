Character Progression

Stat Allocation

You start at Level 1 with 14 stat points. No single stat may exceed 7 at creation. Each new level grants +3 points. The single‑stat cap rises by +1 every two levels (Lv 1–2 → 7, Lv 3–4 → 8, Lv 5–6 → 9, Lv 7+ → 10).

in any stat at creation. Max stat value is 10. Each level thereafter grants +1 point.

WIS+1 Max Mana & +0.5 MP regen each round

Resource Formulae

  • Max HP = CON × 5
  • Max Stamina = 6 + END
  • Max Mana = 6 + WIS
  • Stamina Regen = END per round
  • Mana Regen = 0.5 × WIS per round
