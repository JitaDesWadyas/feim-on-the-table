FEIM: On the Table

FEIM: On the Table is a fantasy tabletop RPG that merges real‑time tension with classic stat‑driven play. Every round equals three in‑world seconds; actions burn TIME and your resource bars (Stamina, Mana). No fixed classes — your hero grows by the things they actually do: swing swords, sling spells, survive brutal fights.

  • Timing matters: act, then the turn jumps to whoever holds the most TIME left.
  • Active defense: Dodge, Parry, Block — or take the hit.
  • Free progression: six core stats, learn any skill or spell you find.
